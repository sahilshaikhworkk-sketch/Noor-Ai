✦ Beginner Tips

🔤 Start with Alif — Arabic is read right to left. Each letter can change shape based on its position in a word.

🎵 Tajweed Rules — Tajweed means reciting the Quran with proper pronunciation. Start slowly and listen to reciters like Sheikh Al-Ghamdi.

📿 Practice Daily — Even 5 minutes a day will build your skills. The Prophet ﷺ said: "The best of you are those who learn the Quran and teach it."
